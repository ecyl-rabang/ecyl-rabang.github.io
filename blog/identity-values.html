Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

I am a product of my experiences and the experiences of others. Growing up as a Filipino, religion is a major part of our identity for a lot of us. It highly influenced our values and morals. They say to always have God as our focus and to give everything up to Him. They say to look for a God-fearing partner. To seek out someone who will have religion and family as their core, and although I don’t disagree, I grew up despising religion. The way it made me feel guilty for not being what they preached I should be. Looking up to those preachers only for them to do the opposite. It disgusted me.

Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

I am a dreamer. I like imagining what my life will be like in the future and completely get lost in it. I believe this will help me manifest this into existence as it acts as a motivation.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

A limitation of mine are my bad habits such as going to sleep really late and waking up late. These could affect my learning journey as it reduces my rest time at night. If I am trying to get things done at night due to my lack of productivity at night, I gotta make up for it at night which isn’t ideal. Another limitation is anxiety. I don’t do well in group settings full of people I have yet to get to know, and it’s scary thinking about starting bootcamp and leaving the comfort of my room.
