Identities and Values

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

I don't know when I made an ethical decision but I do remember quitting uni was a hard decision to make. I had to take into consideration not just my values but my parent's and my culture's values. It's coomon for us to follow the traditional route and finish uni, and since my parents didn't attend a university, they wanted me to achieve that. But I had to put my interest before theirs but I compromised by still furthering my studies through Dev Academy. Since I valued wealth, that played a huge part on my decision. I wanted to be financially stable and going through the university route was taking too long and it was just make me depressed and so I stopped. Another value that helped with my decision was time. I wanted more time for myself and my loved ones and for the things I want to do, and so I figured it's best to get out of uni and think of another way to achieve my goals in life.

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

I am a product of my experiences and the experiences of others. Growing up as a Filipino, religion is a major part of our identity for a lot of us. It highly influenced our values and morals. They say to always have God as our focus and to give everything up to Him and although I don’t entirely disagree, I grew up despising religion. I hated how it made me feel guilty for not being what they preached I should be, only to see the same people not following what they're preaching as well. It made me ponder on the questions "am I a bad person?", "how do you determine if a person is bad?" etc. I hated who I was becoming because of those quesions and so I don't value religion as I did when I was younger.

I am a dreamer. I like imagining what my life will be like in the future and completely get lost in it. I believe this will help me manifest this into existence as it acts as a motivation.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

A limitation of mine are my bad habits such as going to sleep really late and waking up late. These could affect my learning journey as it reduces my rest time at night. If I am trying to get things done at night due to my lack of productivity at night, I gotta make up for it at night which isn’t ideal. Another limitation is anxiety. I don’t do well in group settings full of people I have yet to get to know, and it’s scary thinking about starting bootcamp and leaving the comfort of my room.
